Sound Files for FruitBox Flex Game

The following sound files should be placed in this directory:

1. success.mp3 - A short success sound to play when a level is completed
2. wrong.mp3 - A short error sound to play when an incorrect solution is submitted
3. levelComplete.mp3 - A celebratory sound to play when a level is completed
4. gameComplete.mp3 - A triumphant sound to play when all levels are completed

These sound files should be in MP3 format and kept short (1-2 seconds) for quick feedback.
